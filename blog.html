Geopark Ciletuh

Geopark Ciletuh is one of the UNESCO Global Geoparks located in Sukabumi, West Java. This 128-hectare area holds a number of interesting natural attractions. Starting from the beach, waterfalls, to the hills, all the panoramas look very exotic. Ciletuh Geopark is suitable to be visited for nature lovers.
